The main idea



OneHand is the largest technology platform for crowdfunding in the healthcare sector of Pakistan. We offer a complete range of free healthcare services including medical and surgical treatments, medical camps and tele-health facility to the underprivileged community of Pakistan. Our goal is to reach the millions of needy patients all over Pakistan who are suffering because of lack of healthcare facilities. We continue to serve the humanity at large by providing the best healthcare in Pakistan..
